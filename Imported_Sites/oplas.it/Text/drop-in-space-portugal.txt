PROGRAMM

Wednesday 21th

Arriving in Porto / Famalicão

Thursday 22th

09:30h– Departure to Amorosa beach or to the beach of Capela do Senhor da Pedra in Vila Nova de Gaia, depending on the tides

10:00h – Briefing with participants

10:30 – Start the walk on the beach. Stops to go for a swim.

14:00​h – Lunch at Amorosa or Miramar beach, or pic-nic on the beach

Afternoon free

Friday 23th

10:00h– Departure to Guimarães

10:30h – Visit Centro Cultural Vila Flor Contemporary Arts Museum, theatre & Gardens

13:00​h – Lunch in Guimarães tipical restaurant

15:00h​ – 17:00h – @ dance studio for working on the final restitution ( PT and IT )

Saturday 24th

08:30h– Departure to Gerês

09:30h – Briefing with participants

10:00h – Start trekking to the Big Tree or 7 Lagoons

13:00​h – Pic-nic

14:30h​ – 18:00h – Trekking to the river and waterfalls

19:30h – Return to Famalicão and dinner

Sunday 25th

DAY OFF

Monday 26th

10:00h– Departure to Porto

10:45h – Visit Serralves Contemporary Arts Museum, House & Gardens

14:00​h – Lunch at Serralves Bar/restaurant

15:00h​ – 17:00h – @ dance studio for working on the final restitution ( PT and IT )

Tuesday 27th

10:00h - 12:00h @ dance studio for working on the final restitution ( PT and IT )

18:00h -  Restitution ( place to be defined ) of the work-in-progress with the artistic experiences lived along the project until that day

Wednesday 28th
