My heart is a forest

​​

An immersive idea, an original fable that transports the inner theatre to the dreamlike world of fantasy that is born of the eyes;

the intangible proof that Innocence, Courage and Imagination are able to defeat the fears in every one of us. An indefinable non-place appears like a state of half sleep, begins to perceive sounds, lights from beneath the eyelids, an intermediate space where one awaits only what is imagined.
